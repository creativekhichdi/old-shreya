Leadership Team

Meet The Leaders Who Set The Tone.

Our People Are The Best Of The Best & Expertly Trained Team Members!

Our global logistics expertise, advanced supply chain technology & customized logistics solutions will help you analyze, develop and implement successful supply chain management strategies.

Mr. Sujit Kumar Singh

Founder, Shreya Life Sciences

Mr. Sujit Kumar Singh comes from a pharmaceutical background, earning his degree of medicine in Russia. From there started a small business of trading and distribution of pharmaceutical formulations, that went on to become Shreya Life Sciences as a pharma trading company in the next 5 years. For his contributions to the industry, he was awarded the Rajiv Gandhi Young Entrepreneur Award in 2008.

Shekhar Singh

Head & CEO - Russia Operations

With 30 years of experience as an M-Tech (MADI) Engineer, Mr. Shekhar Singh is a technical expert and a natural leader. Combining his aggressive marketing approach with a gentle personality, he has motivated and guided various teams to success. He has been an integral part of Shreya Life Science, Moscow since its inception.

Dr. K. Chandrasekaran

Head - Business Development & Contract Manufacturing

Dr. K. Chandrasekaran is armed with a Ph.D in Pharmacology & Toxicology and possesses over 28 years of experience in both clinical studies and business development. His entrepreneurial mindset and commitment to success makes him a driving force in the pharmaceutical industry. He has been an integral part of Shreya Life Science since 2002 and has contributed greatly to the company's growth and success.

Avinash Sharma

Head - Emerging & ROW Markets

Mr. Avinash Sharma is a corporate management expert with 21 years of experience across pharmaceuticals and international trade in his arsenal. His expertise in marketing and management, has enabled Shreya Life Science to successfully set up networks across India, the UAE and other countries. His ability to navigate complex regulatory environments and build strong relationships with key stakeholders has been instrumental in our success.
