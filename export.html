Exports Marketing

Exports

At Shreya Life Sciences, our utmost priorities for exporting pharmaceutical products is driven by providing trusted, reliable, safe and high-quality products that improve the health and well-being of people.
We currently export a wide range of formulations and other pharma products to a multitude of countries across five continents.

Key highlights of our International Exports include:

  • High export volumes to Russia & CIS Countries with Headquarters at Moscow, Russia.

  • Export of in-house products to African and far-east countries.

  • Many Shreya products are registered in these countries.

Our Entire Scope of International Exports

Russias

- Operations are set up across the country.
- Products are exported in both Prescription & OTC Segments.
- Over 55 products registered & marked by 160 field force Distribution through six nationally operating distributors.
- Caters to 25000 retail pharmacies (APTEKAS).
- Products distributed through the Public Distribution System of the Russian Govt.

Africa

- Shreya Life Sciences began operations in this continent through Ethiopia.
- Current operations include Kenya, Uganda, DRC Congo & Ethiopia.
- Approval to establish plants in Nigeria is currently under process.
- Our Lozenges is under registration in Morocco.

Kazakhstan

- Partnership with ASNA: a large chain of chemist stores for product marketing with over 3000 retail shops in the country.
- 15 products of Shreya Life Sciences are under registration and expected to start operations soon.

Latin America

- Operations in Cuba, Ecuador, Columbia & Dominican Republic.
- Four products in Ecuador and one product In Cuba are currently under registration.

Uzbekistan

- 35 field staff on ground currently promoting 21 products.
- 5 products of Shreya Life Sciences are under registration in the country.

Myanmar

- 24 products under the label of Shreya are registered.
- Contract Manufacturing with SUN Pharma for sale in Myanmar

Papua New Guinea

- Insulin (Recosulin Brand) is exported to Papua New Guinea.

Domestic Sales & Marketing

At Shreya Life Science, we take pride in our extensive Sales and Marketing set up. At the domestic level, the Shreya Group markets through 3 separate SBUs.

  • Amadeus

  • AkuCare

  • BioLife

Our efficient fleet of field personnels collectively work in collaboration with over 200,000 doctors specialising in all major medical segments. These fields include:

  • General Medicine

  • General Surgery

  • Diabetology

  • Cardiology

  • General Practice

  • Gynaecology

  • Paediatrics

  • Gastroenterology

  • Ophthalmology

  • Oncology
