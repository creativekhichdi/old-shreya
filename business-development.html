Business Development

Contract Manufacturing:

Armed with more than 20+ years of industry expertise, we, at Shreya Life Science, collaborate with pharmaceutical marketing firms on a contract basis to produce and supply formulations, APIs and various medicinal drugs at scale.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is IRA approved while our new facilities are in the process of approval from the European Regulatory Body.

Currently, our illustrious portfolio of clients include globally recognised firms like Lupin, Sun Pharma, Cadila, Piramal and Glenmark among others.

Our international collaborations are lined up with companies from Ukraine, Ethiopia, and Zimbabwe. We welcome contract manufacturing enquiries from both, domestic and international clients.

Kindly send your enquiries to: contractmanufacture@shreya.co.in

Strategic Alliances & Collaborations

Shreya Life Science has a strategic alliance with Bio Technology General Corporation, USA through SciGen, Singapore and Scitech, USA to import and market Recombinant Human Insulin, Hepatitis B vaccine & growth hormone (BTGC acquired by Ferring).

We have a licence agreement with Biotin, Poland to import Recombinant Human Insulin crystals and finished formulation of Recombinant Human Insulin cartridges through SciGen.

We have a licence agreement with Biotech GmBH, Germany (BBT) to market Gonadotropins in India.

We have a licence agreement with Generex Biotechnology Corporation, Canada and the USA to distribute and market Oral Recosulin and 'Oral-Lyn' on an exclusive basis in the Indian sub-continent which includes India, Nepal, Pakistan, Bhutan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka & Myanmar.

We have a licence agreement with Lallemand and Institute Rosell, France to market Probiotics.
